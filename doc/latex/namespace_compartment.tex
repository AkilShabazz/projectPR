\begin{equation} g = 10^6 . \frac{A}{\rho} \end{equation} where $A$ is the compartment area \mbox{[}cm $^2$\mbox{]}, $\rho$ is the specific resistance \mbox{[} $\Omega.cm^2$\mbox{]} and $g$ is the compartment conductance \mbox{[}M\-S\mbox{]}.
